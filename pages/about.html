Our Story

Shema Park (ሽመና ፓርክ) is a cultural weaving organization founded in 2019 (2012 E.C.) and located in Arba Minch, Ethiopia, within the Gamo Zone of Southern Ethiopia. The organization is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich tradition of Ethiopian weaving while empowering local artisans.

At Shema Park, we produce a wide variety of traditional and cultural garments that celebrate the artistry and heritage of the Ethiopian people. From the vibrant patterns to the meticulous craftsmanship, every piece we create is a testament to the beauty of Ethiopian culture.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between tradition and modernity by offering high-quality, handwoven products to the world while supporting the livelihoods of skilled artisans and contributing to cultural preservation.

Our Vision

To become a leading cultural weaving hub that inspires pride in Ethiopian heritage and creates sustainable economic opportunities for artisans in the region.

Why Shema Park?

  • Cultural Preservation: We strive to keep the centuries-old art of weaving alive.
  • Artisan Empowerment: Shema Park provides meaningful employment to local weavers, promoting economic independence.
  • Sustainable Practices: Our operations are rooted in environmentally friendly and ethical practices.

The Weaving Process

Our products are handcrafted using traditional weaving techniques passed down through generations. This process not only ensures the highest quality but also reflects the authenticity and uniqueness of Ethiopian culture.

Our Values

At Shema Park, our core values define who we are and guide our commitment to excellence. We believe in fostering a meaningful impact through our dedication to:

Quality

We take immense pride in the artistry and craftsmanship of our products. Each piece is carefully woven using traditional methods to ensure the highest standards of quality, reflecting the beauty and heritage of Ethiopian culture.

Community

Shema Park thrives on the strength of our community. By empowering local artisans and supporting their livelihoods, we create opportunities for economic independence and nurture a sense of pride in our shared cultural identity.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices in every aspect of our operations. From sourcing sustainable materials to reducing waste, we strive to ensure our impact is positive and long-lasting for future generations.
